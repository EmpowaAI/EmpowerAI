01 — Mindset

Your mission

Use EmpowaAI as if you were genuinely looking for a job today. Don't think like a software tester — think like:

A student about to graduate Someone chasing their first job Someone improving their CV Someone prepping for interviews Someone hunting better opportunities

Use it naturally, then tell us:

  • What felt easy?
  • What felt confusing?
  • What did you expect to happen?
  • What would make you come back and use EmpowaAI again?

That kind of feedback is even more valuable than finding technical bugs.

02 — Setup

Before you begin

Account registration

04 — Set expectations

Expected behaviour (not bugs)

AI takes 20–40 seconds

CV Analysis, CV Revamp, the Digital Twin, Interview Coach, and Simulations aren't instant. Let the request finish — it's working, not frozen.

First Opportunities load is slower

Jobs are fetched live and matched to your profile the first time, then cached. Later loads are much faster.

Sample CVs are fine

You don't need your personal CV — any sample resume works for testing.

It's a beta

You may hit minor UI quirks, small bugs, or slow AI responses. Those are exactly what we're here to find and fix.

05 — Feedback

Reporting bugs

If you hit a bug, tell us as much as you can — this template makes it easy:

What were you trying to do?

"I clicked Analyse CV after uploading my document."

What happened?

"The loading spinner never stopped."

What did you expect?

"I expected the AI analysis to appear."

Anything else?

A screenshot or recording, your browser, and your device all help.

Open bugs at github.com/EmpowaAI/EmpowerAI/issues. Found a security issue? Please don't post it publicly — email nene171408@gmail.com.
