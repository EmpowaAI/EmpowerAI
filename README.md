## 🌟 What is EmpowerAI?

Imagine if you could **see your future** before it happens. What if you could **simulate different career paths** and watch your income grow over time? What if you had an **AI-powered coach** guiding you every step of the way?

**That's EmpowerAI.**

We've created South Africa's first **Digital Economic Twin** – a living, breathing AI model that mirrors your economic potential. It's not just a career tool. It's your **crystal ball** for financial success, specifically designed for the South African youth market.

### 🎯 The Problem We Solve

South Africa faces **63% youth unemployment**. Millions of young people face the same question: *"What should I do with my life?"*

- ❌ No clear path to economic independence
- ❌ Limited visibility into future earning potential  
- ❌ Lack of personalized career guidance
- ❌ CVs that don't stand out in the SA market
- ❌ Interview anxiety and unpreparedness
- ❌ Disconnection from real opportunities

### ✨ The EmpowerAI Solution

We don't just tell you what to do – **we show you what's possible.**

Through AI-powered simulations, personalized coaching, and real South African market data, we empower youth to make informed career decisions and unlock their economic potential.

## 🎨 Core Features

### 🔮 Digital Economic Twin
Your personalized AI avatar that simulates multiple economic futures based on your skills, education, and choices. Watch your empowerment score grow as you level up in your career journey.

**Key Capabilities:**
- 8-dimension CV scoring system
- ATS compatibility analysis
- SA market salary insights (by province)
- Skills gap identification
- Career trajectory predictions

### 📊 Path Simulation Engine
**See your future income** with 3, 6, and 12-month projections across 6 different career pathways:
- 🎓 **Learnerships** - Government-funded training programs
- 💼 **Freelancing** - Independent contractor opportunities
- 📚 **Short Courses** - Quick upskilling paths
- 💻 **Entry-Level Tech** - Software development careers
- 🏢 **Internships** - Corporate training positions
- 🎯 **Graduate Programs** - Structured graduate schemes

### 🗺️ Economic Roadmap
A visual journey map showing exactly what steps to take, which milestones to hit, and what skills you need to develop. **Your GPS to success** in the South African job market.

### 📝 AI CV Analyzer (Premium Feature)
Upload your CV and watch our AI:
- ✂️ Extract your skills automatically (50+ skill categories)
- 🔍 Identify gaps vs. job requirements
- 💡 Generate improvement suggestions
- ✨ Create an ATS-optimized version
- 📊 8-dimension scoring (Content, Format, ATS, Keywords, Experience, Education, Impact, SA Market Fit)
- 💰 SA salary insights by province

### 🎯 Smart Opportunity Matching
Get matched with real opportunities in South Africa – learnerships, bursaries, internships, and entry-level positions tailored to your profile.

**Advanced Filters:**
- Province (Gauteng, Western Cape, KZN, etc.)
- Experience level (Graduate, Junior, Mid, Senior)
- Salary range (R15k - R80k+)
- Remote/Hybrid/On-site options
- Industry sectors

### 🎤 Voice-Enabled Interview Coach
Practice makes perfect. Our AI interview coach with **voice features**:
- 🎭 Simulates real interviews (tech, behavioral, non-tech)
- 🎙️ Voice recognition and synthesis (Web Speech API)
- 📈 Provides instant feedback and scoring
- 🎯 Adapts to company-specific questions
- 📊 Tracks your improvement over time
- 🗣️ Real-time speaking pace and clarity analysis

### 🎮 Gamification System
Stay motivated with XP, levels, and achievements:
- 🥉 Bronze → 🥈 Silver → 🥇 Gold → 💎 Platinum → 💠 Diamond
- Earn XP for completing tasks (CV upload, interview practice, profile updates)
- Unlock achievements and climb the leaderboard
- 7-day and 30-day streaks

### 🤖 24/7 AI Mentor
Get personalized career guidance anytime:
- Career planning advice
- Job search strategies for SA market
- CV and resume reviews
- Interview preparation tips
- Salary negotiation guidance
- SA-specific insights (BEE, employment equity, POPI compliance)

## 🚀 Quick Start

### Prerequisites

- **Node.js** 18+ and npm
- **Python** 3.10+
- **MongoDB Atlas** account (or local MongoDB)
- **OpenAI API Key** (for AI features)
- **Brevo API Key** (for email features)

### Installation

**1. Clone the repository**
```bash
git clone https://github.com/NickiMash17/EmpowerAI.git
cd EmpowerAI
```

**2. Install all dependencies**
```bash
npm run install:all
```

**3. Set up environment variables**

Create `.env` files in each service directory:

**Frontend (`frontend/.env`):**
```env
VITE_API_URL=http://localhost:5000/api
```

**Backend (`empowerai-backend/.env`):**
```env
PORT=5000
MONGODB_URI=your_mongodb_connection_string
JWT_SECRET=your_super_secret_jwt_key
AI_SERVICE_URL=http://localhost:8000/api
BREVO_API_KEY=your_brevo_api_key
FRONTEND_URL=http://localhost:5173
```

**AI Service (`ai-service/.env`):**
```env
OPENAI_API_KEY=your_openai_api_key
BACKEND_URL=http://localhost:5000/api
```

**4. Launch everything**
```bash
npm run dev:all
```

🎉 **That's it!** Your services are now running:
- Frontend: http://localhost:5173
- Backend: http://localhost:5000
- AI Service: http://localhost:8000
