## 🧠 3. CORE FEATURES

### Feature 1: Premium CV Analyzer

**8-Dimension Scoring System**:
1. **Content Quality** (0-100): Relevance, depth, achievements
2. **Format & Structure** (0-100): Layout, sections, hierarchy
3. **ATS Compatibility** (0-100): Keywords, parsing, format
4. **Keyword Density** (0-100): Industry terms, skills, verbs
5. **Experience Showcase** (0-100): Quantifiable results, metrics
6. **Education & Credentials** (0-100): Qualifications, certifications
7. **Impact & Achievements** (0-100): Measurable outcomes, awards
8. **SA Market Fit** (0-100): Local context, SA companies, provinces

**SA Market Intelligence**:
- Salary ranges (Entry, Mid, Senior)
- Top companies hiring in SA
- Best provinces for opportunities
- Skills to add for salary boost

**Export Options**:
- PDF Report (Professional, branded)
- ATS-Optimized DOCX
- One-Page Summary
- LinkedIn Format

---

### Feature 2: Voice Interview Coach

**Real-time AI Feedback**:
- Speech recognition
- Confidence scoring (0-100%)
- Clarity analysis
- Speaking pace tracking (WPM)
- Emotion detection

**Question Bank**:
- Technical questions
- Behavioral questions (STAR method)
- SA-specific questions (BEE, diversity, POPI)
- Company-specific questions

**Practice Modes**:
1. Quick Practice (5 questions, 10 mins)
2. Full Interview (15 questions, 45 mins)
3. Company-Specific prep
4. Weakness Training
5. Mock Panel Interview

**Performance Analytics**:
- Interview Readiness score
- Strengths & weaknesses
- Recommended practice areas

---

### Feature 3: 24/7 AI Mentor

**Capabilities**:
1. Career Planning ("What path should I follow?")
2. Job Search Strategy ("Where to apply in Cape Town?")
3. CV/Resume Help ("Review my experience section")
4. Interview Prep ("Common questions for this company?")
5. Salary Negotiation ("How to negotiate 20% raise?")
6. SA-Specific Guidance ("BEE requirements?")
7. Emotional Support ("Feeling discouraged after rejections")

**Context Awareness**:
- Remembers user profile (skills, experience, goals)
- Tracks conversation history (last 10 chats)
- Provides personalized insights
- Suggests actionable next steps

**Leaderboard**:
- Weekly, Monthly, All-Time views
- Provincial filters
- Top 100 users displayed
- Your rank + XP to next rank

### Feature 5: Smart SA Opportunities

**AI Matching Algorithm** (0-100% match):
- 40% Skills overlap
- 25% Experience level
- 15% Education match
- 10% Location proximity
- 10% Career goals alignment

**Filters**:
- Province (Gauteng, Western Cape, KZN, etc.)
- Type (Jobs, Internships, Learnerships, Bursaries)
- Salary range (R15k - R80k+)
- Experience level (Graduate, Junior, Mid, Senior)
- Remote options (Remote, Hybrid, On-site)
- Industry sectors

**Opportunity Card Shows**:
- Match percentage + breakdown
- Why you're a great fit

- Companies that hired EmpowerAI users
