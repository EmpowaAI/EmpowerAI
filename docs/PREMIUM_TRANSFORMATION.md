## 🎯 DESIGN PRINCIPLES APPLIED

### **1. Color Philosophy**
- **Primary:** #2563eb (Professional Blue)
- **Accent:** #06b6d4 (Cyber Cyan)
- **Success:** #10b981 (Clean Green)
- **Warning:** #f59e0b (Attention Orange)
- **Error:** #ef4444 (Clear Red)
- **Neutrals:** Premium gray scale (50-900)

### **2. Typography**
- **Font:** Inter with OpenType features
- **Headings:** 800, 700, 600 weights
- **Body:** 400, 500 weights
- **Letter spacing:** -0.02em to -0.011em
- **Line heights:** 1.1 to 1.75

### **3. Spacing**
- **Base unit:** 4px (0.25rem)
- **Scale:** 4, 8, 12, 16, 20, 24, 32, 40, 48, 64, 80, 96px
- **Consistent padding:** 24px (1.5rem) for cards
- **Section spacing:** 64px to 96px

### **4. Shadows**
- **6 levels:** xs, sm, md, lg, xl, 2xl
- **Subtle:** RGBA black with 0.03 to 0.12 opacity
- **Professional depth:** No harsh shadows
- **Dark mode support:** Darker shadows

### **5. Animations**
- **Speed:** 150ms (fast), 200ms (base), 300ms (slow)
- **Easing:** cubic-bezier(0.4, 0, 0.2, 1)
- **Hover:** translateY(-4px) with shadow increase
- **Entrance:** fade-in, slide-up, scale
- **Staggered:** 100ms delay between items
