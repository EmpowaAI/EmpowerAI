# Contributors

EmpowerAI is built by a small team and open source contributors who believe South African youth deserve real tools for economic mobility.

## Core team

| Name | Role |
|---|---|

## How to contribute

We welcome contributions from developers, AI researchers, UX designers, and people with lived experience of South Africa's job market.

See [CONTRIBUTING.md](CONTRIBUTING.md) for setup instructions, coding standards, and the pull request process.
